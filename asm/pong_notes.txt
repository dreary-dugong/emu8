0x2ea: paddle sprite
0x2d4: subroutine to write scores to screen
0x2f0: ball sprite

0x22a: user input -> ai -> paddle drawing -> ball movement -> ball collision

va, vb: left paddle x, y
vc, vd: right paddle x, y

v6: ball x
v7: ball y

v8: ball dx (starts at 2)
v9: ball dy (starts at 255)

ve: score
